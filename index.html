Ever wonder about the historical markers as you travel?

Now you can automatically hear history as you travel.

Android App - BETA TEST NOW AVAILABLE

Automatically speaks the marker information as you drive by. You get choose the radius to speak markers.

Integrates Google Maps

Markers automatically appear as you drive. You have the option to view the marker details or navigate to the marker.

Announces New Markers

As markers appear within your defined distance, the title or complete details of the marker are spoken.

Tracks in Background

You can decide if markers are collected while you use your phone for other things.

Features

Automatically track and hear historical markers as you drive.

Automatic

As you drive, when new markers are detected (within your desired radius) they are announced via voice synthesis. You can turn this off if you like.

Google Maps

Integrates the power of Google Maps to be assured map data is up to date and accurage.

Educational

Learn about the hidden history all around us from the convenience of your vehicle. If you find a marker that sounds interesting, you have plenty of time to stop, turn around and discover stories of bygone days.

No user accounts & No Ads

We strongly dislike the use of Ads and user accounts to collect data. The only data we collect is google-anonymized statistics for unexpected application errors so we can fix them.

We collect no user information. You can use the app without signing up to anything. Purchases are all tracked via the App & Play stores.
